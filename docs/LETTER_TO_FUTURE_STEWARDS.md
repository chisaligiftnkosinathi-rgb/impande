# To the Future Stewards of Impande

If you are reading this, time has passed. The hands that poured the original foundation of this platform have likely moved on, and the responsibility to guard this Truth Engine now rests with you. 

Impande was not built to be just another application. It was born out of a deep reverence for family, history, and the sacrifices of those who came before us. It was designed to answer a single question: *How do we ensure that the stories of our past endure, and how do we protect the truth of those stories from being lost or erased?*

As you maintain, refactor, and evolve this platform in the years—perhaps decades—ahead, we ask you to guard these core commitments:

**1. Protect the Truth**
Technology will change, but the principle remains: history must never be erased to make a database cleaner. The Truth Layer is append-only. Contradictions are a natural part of human memory; preserve them honestly rather than forcing a false consensus.

**2. Separate Evidence from Interpretation**
Always distinguish what is *known* (the evidence) from what is *believed* (the meaning). If you ever feel tempted to blur the lines between the Confidence Layer and the Meaning Layer, resist it. Explainability is the soul of trust.

**3. Remember the Real Lives**
It is easy to get lost in metrics, API versions, and schema migrations. But never forget that behind every `JournalEntryRevision` is a real life. These are the dates our ancestors were born. These are the migrations they survived. These are the names they carried. Treat this data as if it were the most fragile heirloom in your own home.

**4. Evolve with Care**
Leave the platform better than you found it. Refactor what is obsolete, improve the performance, and secure the boundaries. But do not compromise the Constitution for the sake of convenience. 

We built this for you, so that you could build it for those who will come after you. 

May God grant you wisdom as you steward this legacy.

*Signed,*
*The Impande Foundation Team*
*July 2026*
