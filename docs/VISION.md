# Impande Vision

## Why does Impande exist?
Impande exists to preserve legacy. History is fragile—especially family history, oral tradition, and localized community archives. Too often, historical records are lost, corrupted, or digitized into opaque platforms that erase the provenance of the information. Impande is a Truth Engine designed to ensure that knowledge endures, and that future generations know exactly *why* a piece of history is believed to be true.

## What problems is it trying to solve?
1. **The Ephemeral Nature of Oral History:** Capturing stories before they fade.
2. **The Loss of Provenance:** Digital family trees often present facts (e.g., "Born in 1912") without any source, leaving future researchers unable to verify the claim.
3. **The Erasure of Contradiction:** When two people remember a date differently, platforms usually force a single "winner." Impande embraces contradiction, storing both claims and letting confidence metrics dictate the narrative.
4. **Institutional Silos:** Community archives often lack the software needed to federate, collaborate, and securely manage access.

## What principles should never change?
- **Truth is Append-Only:** We never overwrite accepted history. We only append new evidence.
- **Evidence is separate from Interpretation:** A fact (Truth Layer) must never be conflated with the story derived from it (Meaning Layer).
- **Access Governs Stewardship, not Truth:** Permissions only limit who can contribute or review; they do not bend the historical record.
- **Systems Preservation:** An archive is only as good as its ability to survive an outage. Operational resilience is a stewardship duty.

## Expected Evolution
Impande Version 1.0 is a foundation. We expect the platform to evolve by:
- Federating with major international archives and museum databases.
- Expanding the AI Meaning Layer to translate dense provenance graphs into accessible, narrative-driven family biographies.
- Developing deeper Trust Metrics that automatically flag neglected historical eras within a collection.
