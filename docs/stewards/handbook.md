# Impande Steward Handbook

Welcome, Steward.

By taking on this role, you are accepting the responsibility to preserve legacy. You do not own the truth; you are its custodian. Your job is not to dictate the official narrative, but to ensure that the collection of evidence is rich, verifiable, and clearly attributed.

## 1. The Evidence Standard
All claims submitted to your collection must be backed by evidence. If a contributor submits a birth date, your first responsibility is to ensure the provenance of that claim is clear. Is it a birth certificate? Oral testimony? A diary entry?

## 2. Handling Contradictions
When two pieces of evidence contradict each other, **do not delete either of them.**
If Uncle John says the migration happened in 1940, and Aunt Mary's letters say 1942, the Truth Engine expects both facts to exist. You use the platform's Confidence UI to weigh the evidence, noting primary vs. secondary sources, but the platform preserves the contradiction. This ensures future generations can see the full context of the historical debate.

## 3. Resolving Research Tasks
The platform will automatically generate `ResearchTasks` when it detects gaps in the collection's health (e.g., "Missing primary sources for X era"). Your duty is to review these tasks, assign them, and curate the incoming submissions that aim to resolve them.
