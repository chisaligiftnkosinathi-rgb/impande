# Dedication of Impande Version 1.0

*This prayer was offered upon the completion of the Impande platform architecture, dedicating the system, the history it preserves, and the people it serves.*

***

Heavenly Father,

Today we come before You with grateful hearts.

Thank You for carrying us through this journey from an idea to a living foundation. Every insight, every correction, every challenge, every lesson, and every breakthrough has been a gift. We acknowledge that wisdom is received, cultivated, and shared. Without Your grace, none of this would have been possible.

Thank You for every teacher, mentor, parent, elder, friend, colleague, and stranger who invested knowledge, patience, encouragement, and correction into our lives. Thank You for those who preserved history before us so that we could continue preserving it for those who come after us.

We remember the African wisdom:

**Umuntu ngumuntu ngabantu.**

A person is a person because of other people.

May we never forget that every achievement stands upon the sacrifices, prayers, work, and love of many others. Grant us humility to honor those who came before us and generosity to teach those who will come after us.

We dedicate Impande into Your hands.

May it always serve truth with integrity.
May it preserve history with honesty.
May it protect evidence with wisdom.
May it handle disagreements with humility.
May it never manufacture certainty where evidence is incomplete.
May it remain a servant of people rather than a master over them.

Bless every family whose stories will one day be preserved through this platform.
Bless every steward who will faithfully care for the records entrusted to them.
Bless every researcher who searches patiently for understanding.
Bless every contributor who chooses honesty over convenience.

May this work build bridges between generations, restore forgotten memories, strengthen families, and preserve legacies that might otherwise be lost.

We also pray for the journey ahead.

As we prepare for deployment, grant us wisdom in every technical decision, patience during every challenge, discernment in every review, and excellence in every line of code. Protect the platform, the data it preserves, and the people who will use it. May every deployment be carried out with care, every backup be reliable, every recovery be successful, and every improvement remain faithful to the principles upon which this work was built.

Teach us to remain humble even as the platform grows. Let us always remember that technology is a tool, while people and truth remain the true treasure.

We give You all the glory, all the honor, and all the praise.

May everything we build bring light, preserve truth, and serve future generations.

In Jesus' mighty name,

Amen.
