# Code of Conduct

## Core Principles

As a research and innovation ecosystem, we expect all collaborators, contributors, and partners to adhere to the following principles:

* **Respect:** Treat all participants, communities, and scientific records with dignity and respect.
* **Evidence before opinion:** Ensure that discourse, architecture, and governance decisions are grounded in demonstrable facts and logic.
* **Transparency:** Communicate design decisions and operational constraints openly.
* **Scientific integrity:** Uphold the rigor of the scientific method and computational continuity in all implementations.
* **Community benefit:** Strive to build digital infrastructure that ultimately serves societal, economic, and community well-being.
* **Professional collaboration:** Maintain a professional demeanor that welcomes diverse disciplines, from laboratory technicians to software engineers to policymakers.
